Tina S. Zhu

Tina Zhu is a Chinese American writer of fiction about freaks and weirdos. She lives in California. When she’s not hiding among the redwoods or indulging her sweet tooth, she can be found on Twitter at @tinaszhu.

Her blog, with mostly reading updates and occasional writing ones, is http://thebookcave.substack.com.
